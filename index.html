Recommender system causal bias elimination

  1. Independently completed a full-stack industrial recommendation project, including pair requirements, data washing, feature engineering, multi-task model design, causal bias elimination algorithm design, deployment flow cutting, and metrics optimization

  2. ESMM is a classic multi-task recommendation model in the industry. We demonstrate that the CVR estimation of ESMM suffers from an eigenbias problem, and CTCVR estimation also introduces an incorrect conditional independent prior.

  3. To address this problem, ESCM is proposed with the intention of using causal debiasing techniques to regularize the ESMM model and demonstrate that it can significantly mitigate the bias and conditional independence problems.

  4. ESCM was deployed to Alipay insurance product page and insurance strict selection shelf page, and took up the task of booth for insurance business during the five blessings, serving 6 million users in 10 days, improving business billing by 4 percent and actual premium by 8 percent; declaring one patent, one paper and one article on Alibaba Technology Association.

Research on decoupling analysis of micro-Doppler signals for complex dynamic multi-targets

  1. Target dynamic model: based on target microdynamic characteristics, motion characteristics, electromagnetic parameters, the establishment of spatial complex dynamic multi-target motion model under a variety of radar regimes.

  2. Decoupling separation algorithm: decoupling of each motion mode based on variational modal decomposition; separation of different target echoes based on heuristic methods.

  3. Modal classification algorithm: deep learning to achieve extraction of micro-motion features and classification of targets; inter-channel attention mechanism to improve the generalization performance of the model for low signal-to-noise ratio images and achieve SOTA level for micro-motion image recognition; combination of mechanism and data decoding to achieve high-precision identification of model motion parameters.

  4. Project achievements: declared one national invention patent; wrote one thesis; excellent thesis of Central South University; received 20,000 RMB award from supervisor.
